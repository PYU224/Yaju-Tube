Yaju-Tube is a lightweight Peertube video player app.

Features:
- Browse videos from Peertube instances
- Search and filter videos
- Multiple sorting options
- Light/Dark themes
- Multi-language support (English, Japanese, German)
- Add and manage multiple instances

This app was created because the official Peertube app was too resource-intensive. It provides a fast and efficient way to watch Peertube videos on Android.
